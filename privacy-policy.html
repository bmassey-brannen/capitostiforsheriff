Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how The Committee to Elect Brad Capitosti for Burke County Sheriff ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information you provide when interacting with our campaign through this website, email, text messaging, or other digital communications.

When you provide your name, email address, phone number, or other contact information through this website or at campaign events, we may use that information to contact you with campaign updates, event invitations, volunteer opportunities, public service announcements, fundraising appeals, or surveys. If you provide your mobile number and consent to receive text messages, you may receive recurring campaign-related texts from us.

Message frequency may vary based on campaign activity. Message and data rates may apply. You can unsubscribe from text messages at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal contact information with third parties or other campaigns. We may use trusted service providers to help manage campaign communications or platform operations, but those providers are required to keep your information confidential and use it only to support our authorized campaign activities.

By submitting your phone number or email on our website, you consent to receive communications from The Committee to Elect Brad Capitosti for Burke County Sheriff using the contact method(s) you provided. Consent to receive text messages is not required to donate, volunteer, or otherwise participate in the campaign.

We take reasonable steps to protect the security of your information. If you have any questions about this policy or the information we collect, please contact us using the form on our website.

This policy is subject to change. Any updates will be posted on this page. Continued use of this website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
